FAQ/Contact

Frequently Asked Questions Where do I go to access the template catalogue? After clicking on the Get Started link, you must first start a project. Click on the submenu to the left, and click the community tab. Then click on the template tag. Where do I go to learn about using the Website Builder? After starting a project, click on the submenu to the left, then click the question mark icon on the top left corner. How do I upload my own template? If your are finished with your template, click the right submenu, then click publish. On the publish page, select the community bubble. Add a name and description of your template and submit! Your submission will be manually approved by our moderation team. Can I make money off of my website? Yes! With a free license, we take 30% of all profits made through our service.
